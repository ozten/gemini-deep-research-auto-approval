Privacy Policy for Gemini Deep Research Auto Approval

Last updated: July 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Gemini Deep Research Auto Approval ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in connection with our Chrome extension (the "Service").

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) or any other user data. The extension's functionality is entirely client-side, meaning it runs only within your browser.

2. How the Extension Works

The extension's sole purpose is to automate a click on the "Start research" button on the Gemini web application. It does not read, record, or transmit any information from the page or your interactions with it.

3. Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

* `storage`: To save your preference for enabling or disabling the extension.
* `scripting`: To inject the code that performs the button click.
* `host_permissions`: To run only on `https://gemini.google.com/app`.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
